My Not-So-Tragic Backstory

I love to laugh and dancing in the rain— I'm just kidding!
Please don’t let my quiet, serious appearance deceive you. I’m actually a storyteller with a head full of ideas who need to get them out before I explode. And if you ever need me, you can easily find me ready to draw anything and everything on anything and everything.

In other words I'm your basic bitch illustrator with a dream to draw comics, but have you seen DC and Marvel? They're in shambles! I'd rather go my own way. However, I'm not shy to draw for any other indie comics, Nor, am I shy to doing any other illustration from editorials, to motion, to even- gasp- vector!
