Backstory

Alina is an illustrator and comic artist born and raised in Richmond, BC. A recent of Capilano University’s IDEA School of Design, she’s into all things storytelling. Whatever the genre— movies, pulps, mythology— she has a favourite. Naturally, she wants to tell stories too, both other’s and her own. Her strengths include: illustration, storyboarding, generating story prompts, playing piano, daydreaming, and appreciating the classics.
