Backstory

I'm an illustrator and comic artist born and raised in Richmond, BC. Once I get started on a project, I won't stop 'til I'm done. No really, I was one of those kids that actually liked going to Saturday piano lessons. I still play piano. When I'm not illustrating or playing piano, I like to read novels, learning useless info from the web, and remembering to eat my lunch.
