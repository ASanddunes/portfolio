Hockey In After Effects

Motion Illustration

The point of this project was to illustrate a scene with motion. The prompt was Canada, and I'm a sucker for hockey.

Initially I hd laid out the how each figure and piece in the illustration would move in the storyboard. Specifically I had a camera that would zoom out from the logo of both player's jerseys, letting the viewers know the context. And once the camera capture the full scene, the Canadian player would move forward to with the puck to capture how the Canadian player has ownership of the ppuck over the Swedish player in the back, whose effort to chase after the puck was ultimately futile. In the meanwhile, the hockey players in the back are further left behind, shrinking into the horizon.
